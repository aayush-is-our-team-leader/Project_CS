# Team Working Agreement

---

## Culture

### Trust: Foundation of Openness
All team members are expected to demonstrate **honesty, effort, trustworthiness, and competency** in all interactions. By maintaining transparency and reliability, each member contributes to a safe environment where vulnerability and candid communication are encouraged without fear of judgment or reprisal.

### Conflict: Passionate Debate of Ideas
Team members will engage in discussions with **strong communication skills, empathy, and outspokenness**. Debates will remain constructive and focused on ideas rather than individuals, ensuring the team identifies the best technical and process solutions.

### Commitment: Buy-In and Clarity
All team members will show **commitment and amicability** when participating in decisions. After all voices have been heard, members will honor the group’s decisions, even if full consensus is not reached, ensuring clarity and buy-in from the entire team.

### Accountability: Peer-to-Peer Standards
Team members will uphold **respect, responsibility, and accountability** in maintaining high standards. Each member is responsible for monitoring both their own and the team’s contributions, reinforcing peer-to-peer accountability rather than relying on external facilitators.

### Results: Our Collective Goal
Members will demonstrate **friendliness and a strong goal-oriented mindset**, prioritizing the team’s objectives above individual recognition or status. All contributions will focus on achieving collective results while maintaining a supportive team environment.

---

## Meetings

- **Meeting Schedule:** Meetings will occur on **Tuesdays and Thursdays from 8:00 AM to 10:00 AM on campus**, with additional **online synchronous meetings via Discord from 8:00 PM to 9:00 PM**.
- **Meeting Location:** Campus classrooms during in-class sessions and Discord for online sessions.
- **Note-Taker:** **Devna** will document all discussions, decisions, and action items.
- **Storage Location for Notes:** Meeting notes will be stored on **Google Drive** and **GitHub**.
- **Meeting Format:** Meetings are **free spoken and guided by assignment priorities**, rather than following a formal structure.
- **Work Item Follow-Up Process:** Tasks and responsibilities will be tracked through **GitHub** and documented in a **Contribution.md file**, which will be updated regularly to reflect progress.
- **Protocol for Absence:** Members unable to attend a meeting must **notify the team via Discord with an @ mention**, providing a **summary of the meeting, including links to notes and relevant assignment criteria**.
- **Consequences for Missing or Late Attendance:** Members who are absent or late will meet **1:1 with the team leader, Aayush**, and may be escalated to the **professor** if necessary.

---

## Communication

- **Communication Channels:** The team will use **GitHub** for project collaboration and task tracking, and **Discord** for real-time messaging and discussions.
- **Expected Communication Pattern:** Team members will communicate via the **team channel** for general discussions, **direct messaging** for individual or urgent matters, and a **progress-update channel** for reporting task status and project milestones.
- **Response Time Expectations:** All messages should receive a response within **8–12 hours** to ensure timely coordination.

---

## Decisions

Decisions will be reached through **discussion and group consensus**. If the team encounters an even split or cannot reach consensus, the decision will be made either by a **coin flip** or by **elevating the matter to the professor**.

---

## Development

- **Work Allocation:** Tasks will be assigned based on each member’s **preferences and skills**, with allocation decided through **mutual agreement** to ensure equitable distribution of responsibilities.
- **Work Completion Process:** Most work will be **completed independently**, but **pair or team collaborations** will be planned collaboratively when required.
- **Consequences for Incomplete or Incorrect Work:** Incomplete tasks may be **redistributed among remaining team members**, and persistent issues may be **escalated to the team leader or professor** as necessary.

---

## Workflow

- **Task Board Usage:** The task board will leverage **AI to reference relevant parts of the working agreement**, providing reminders and clarification to guide task management.
- **Issue Creation:** **Any team member** may create an issue. All issues will be **considered, resolved, or escalated during group meetings**.
- **Issue Movement:** Issues will be moved when work is incomplete by the deadline or after evaluation by the group. The **team leader will move issues** based on **group consensus**.
- **Procedure for Moving Issues:** Issues are updated during **group meetings**, and the **assignee is notified via @ mention on Discord**.

---

## Agreement Review

The working agreement is a **living document** and will be reviewed at the **start of every meeting**. **AI will summarize the relevant sections** of the agreement in relation to the topics being discussed, ensuring alignment and clarity throughout the project.
