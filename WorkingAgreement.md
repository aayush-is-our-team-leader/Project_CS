Culture
In this section, you will define the culture of your team reflecting the agile peer-team model structure. Provide 2-4 positive characteristics that all team members should bring to all work as bullets under each of the following sub-sections.

Trust: Foundation of Openness (We define trust as the ability to be vulnerable with one another without fear of judgment or reprisal.)

Conflict: Passionate Debate of Ideas (We engage in “unfiltered and passionate debate” to ensure we find the best technical and process solutions.)

Commitment: Buy-In and Clarity (We commit to decisions once all voice have been “heard and considered,” even when total consensus is not reached.)

Accountability: Peer-to-Peer Standards (We accept that the primary responsibility for maintaining high standards rests with us as peers, not with an outside facilitator.)

Results: Our Collective Goal (We prioritize the “collective goals of the group” over our individual status, egos, or specialized roles.)
